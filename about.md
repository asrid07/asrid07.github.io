# About TrendPulse Daily

**TrendPulse Daily** is a multi-niche blog covering the latest trends across **Technology, Finance, Health, Science, Business, Entertainment, Education, and Lifestyle**.

We scan hundreds of sources daily — including Hacker News, Reddit, Dev.to, GitHub, Google Trends, BBC, and TechCrunch — to bring you the most relevant, well-researched articles on what's happening right now.

## What We Cover

- 🖥️ **Technology** — AI, programming, cybersecurity, gadgets
- 💰 **Finance** — Investing, crypto, personal finance, market trends
- 🏥 **Health** — Wellness, medical breakthroughs, mental health
- 🔬 **Science** — Space, physics, environmental science, discoveries
- 📈 **Business** — Startups, strategy, remote work, entrepreneurship
- 🎬 **Entertainment** — Movies, gaming, streaming, pop culture
- 📚 **Education** — Online learning, career skills, study tips
- 🌍 **Lifestyle** — Travel, productivity, relationships, self-improvement

## Transparency

- We update content regularly to ensure relevance
- All data comes from verified, reputable sources
